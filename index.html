Privacy Policy — Figyr

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Figyr is a personal canvas app for notes and drawings. We built it with one rule: your content stays on your device.

Data We Collect

None.

Figyr does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers.

How Your Data Is Stored

Everything you create in Figyr — notes, drawings, canvas layouts — is saved exclusively in a local database on your device using on-device SQLite storage. This data never leaves your phone.

When you delete the app, all of your data is permanently deleted along with it.

Third-Party Services

Figyr does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Permissions

Figyr does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

Children's Privacy

Figyr does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
