Privacy Policy — Figyr

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Figyr is a personal canvas app for capturing notes and drawings. By default, everything you create stays on your device. You can optionally create an account to sync your content across multiple devices.

Data We Collect

Without an account: Figyr collects no personal data. All content is stored locally on your device.

With an account (optional): When you sign in with Apple, we receive the following:

  • A unique user ID
  • Your name provided on first sign-in
  • Your email address

This information is used solely to identify your account and sync your content across your devices. We do not sell or share it with third parties.

How Your Content Is Stored

Everything you create in Figyr — notes, drawings, canvas layouts — is saved in a local database on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you sign in to enable sync.

If you are not signed in, deleting the app permanently removes all of your data.

Cloud Sync (Signed-In Users Only)

If you create an account, your canvases and notes (including text, drawings, and link previews) are encrypted in transit and stored securely on our servers. This allows your content to sync across your devices. Your content is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users.

Signing out of Figyr does not delete your data from the server. If you want to permanently delete your account and all associated data, use the Delete Account option in the app's account settings.

Link Previews

When you add a link to a canvas, Figyr fetches a preview (title, description, thumbnail) from that URL. For links from Twitter/X and TikTok, this request goes through their official oEmbed APIs. For other URLs, the page is fetched directly. Your IP address will be visible to those external services when this happens. Preview data is cached locally and synced to your account if you are signed in.

Third-Party Services

Figyr uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign-In — optional sign-in method
  • Twitter/X and TikTok oEmbed APIs — link preview metadata (when you add those links)

Figyr does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or behavioral tracking services.

Permissions

Figyr does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

Children's Privacy

Figyr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
